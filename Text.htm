Text

This is the most crowded part of the program. You can do several things to the text here.

First type in your text in the large text box.. Next select the font you want to use. Then Select the color of the font. Decide if you want bold, italic and/or underlined and set the font size.

If your font color is something other than black you can choose to put a shadow under the text (well, you can use a black font but you will not see it). Some darker colors don't show the shadow too good. If you want a shadow then click the "Shadow" check box and type in the offset (right and down) you want.

Alignment

  • The top, mid and bottom is the vertical position
  • The left, center and right is the horizontal position

Move Text

  • The move buttons moves the text by 2 pixels.
  • The text boxes are an absolute move to a position
