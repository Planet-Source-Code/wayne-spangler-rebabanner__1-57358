Border

A border can be placed around the banner. It can be solid or shaded.

If you want a broder click on the "Show" checkbox.

Put in the size of your border in the "Width" box. The width is in pixels.

If you want a line around the banner then click the "Line" option button.

  • A button appears when the "Line" option button is selected. It is used to select the color of the line.

If you want a shaded border around your banner click on the "Shade" option

  • The light and dark controls appear when the "Shade" option button appears.
  • There are two sets of controls. The light border which is the left and top sides and the dark border which is the right and bottom sides.
  • The lighter lightens each pixel in the width of the border of the sides corrasponding to that control by 10 percent.
  • The darker does the same.
