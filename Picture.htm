Picture

If you want a picture on your banner click on "Show" and then press "Load Picture" button and select your picture.

You can resize your picture using the arrows. How much they are changed is the text box marked "Resize Amount"

You can move your picture with the arrows. Put a number in the "Move Distance" to control how far each click moves the picture. This is in pixels.

In case you change the size of the banner and the picture disappears press the "Move Picture to Top" button to move the picture to the top left corner of the banner
